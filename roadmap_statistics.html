Roadmap to get into Statistics

Technician Skills

Some of the technical skills you may require are:

  • Statistical Methods
  • Linear Algebra
  • Data Mining
  • Data Visualisation
  • Wordpress

Programming Skills

Specifically, most statisticians must know one or more programming languages and/or specialized statistical software. Examples of these are:

Programming languages: R, Python, Java, Perl, Julia.

Specialized software: SAS, SPSS, Matlab.

Database software: SQL Server, PostgreSQL, and NoSQL.

  • Python
  • Matlab
  • NoSQL

Soft Skills

Some of the soft skills you may require as a statistician are:

  • Communication
  • Teamwork
  • Curiosity

Career Paths

Statisticians are employed in a variety of different fields and industries. However, these are the primary industries that maintain a high demand for statisticians:

  • Research & academia
  • Government
  • Healthcare
  • Sports
